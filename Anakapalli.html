A small trip to Anakapalli and Surroundings

It was january Post Pongal and our Friend JanakiRam came from UK. He planned to visit Araku lambasingi places, so he planned with a group of guys named DKanth, Manchi Baludu Manoj, MLA maya praveen and with me. And finally the Day Came. We reached MLA maya house in Anakapalli and we stayed there for a night. In the early morning We started to Lambasingi by road and then to kothapalli water falls, Paderu Araku and then to Borra Caves. It was really a wonderful trip. Apart from the trip it was a great to spennd some quality time with our friends together after a long time. Hope many such trips will happen soon in the near future.
